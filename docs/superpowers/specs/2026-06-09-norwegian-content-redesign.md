# Norwegian Content Redesign — Al Rawdah Institutt

**Date:** 2026-06-09
**Scope:** Rewrite all landing page copy in Norwegian for Norwegian Muslim youth

---

## Audience
Norwegian Muslim youth. Some prior Islamic knowledge, most have grown up in Norway with limited access to structured Islamic education. They want to understand their religion properly — not just hear about it secondhand.

## Tone
Warm and accessible (no prior knowledge assumed) + direct and serious about Arabic as the real unlock. Not preachy. Not dumbed down. Treats youth as capable of genuine scholarship.

## Core message
Arabic is the key to Islam. Everything else follows from that. Al Rawdah gives you the structure to actually get there.

## Main differentiator
Structure — a real curriculum with levels, progression, and teacher feedback. Not random YouTube videos.
