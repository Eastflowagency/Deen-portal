Når du trykker på Nivå 1 — where does it go?

The "Slik fungerer det" table rows will become clickable. Which page should they navigate to?

A

→ Studieplan (pre-selected to that level)

Clicking "Nivå 1" opens /studieplan with Nivå 1 tab already active — shows all 8 courses for that year side by side. Best for parents and new students who want a full year overview.

URL: /studieplan?nivå=1
B

→ Kurs (filtered to that level)

Clicking "Nivå 1" opens /kurs with the Nivå 1 filter tab pre-selected — shows all subjects with only their Grunnivå course visible. Good for comparing subjects at the same level.

URL: /kurs?filter=1
C

→ Both — Nivå row goes to Studieplan, Subject cards go to Kurs

Split the navigation intent: Nivå rows (time-based) go to Studieplan. Subject cards (topic-based) in "Hva du vil lære" go to /kurs. Two different entry points for two different mental models.

Recommended — matches user intent most naturally
