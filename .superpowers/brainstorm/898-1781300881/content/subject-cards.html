How should the subject cards connect to /kurs?

Currently the cards in "Hva du vil lære" are static. They'll now link to /kurs. Pick the treatment.

Each card wraps in a Link. A small "Se kurs →" label appears at the bottom of every card. Whole card is the tap target — good for mobile.
